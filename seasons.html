Season 1 Current Season

Season 1 has officially begun! This season delivers the intense LifeSteal experience — every time you take down another player, you steal one of their hearts, growing stronger with each victory. But survival isn’t just about killing… you can also craft hearts using rare materials to restore your life, and even revive fallen players if you choose to bring them back into the fight. Form alliances, plan betrayals, and battle for dominance in a world where every heart counts. Lose them all, and you’re gone for good — unless someone decides you’re worth reviving.
